Weather Analysis Compare two cities

Data source: https://rdrr.io/cran/rattle.data/man/weatherAUS.html

Data Format: CSV file

Columns in the Dataset :

  1. Date
  2. Location
  3. Temperature
  4. Rainfall
  5. Evaporation
  6. Sunshine
  7. Wind direction
  8. Wind speed
  9. Humidity
  10. Pressure
  11. Cloudiness
  12. Chance of rain


Dataset contains the following cities as shown in the map below:







The following analyses will be performed on the selected cities of the dataset:

  • How is the effect of temperature changes on other weather parameters like rainfall, humidity, evaporation and pressure?
  • What is the advice for certain industries who are the most affected by the weather changes?

The aim of this study is to give advice on those industrial sectors which are mostly affected by weather changes. These sectors are:

  • Agriculture
  • Insurance
  • Tourism
